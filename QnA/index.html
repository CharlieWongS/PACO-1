Questions & Answers

What affects the accuracy of location tracking?
The accuracy of location tracking depends on the quality of the GPS signal. In environments such as underground parking garages or areas surrounded by tall buildings, location data may be less precise.
Where is my data stored, and can it be recovered if deleted?
All data is securely stored locally on your device (iPhone/iPad). Please note that once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
How can I ensure the app records parking locations automatically?
To ensure seamless tracking, we recommend enabling “Always” location access in your device settings. This allows the app to automatically record parking locations even when it is not actively in use.
